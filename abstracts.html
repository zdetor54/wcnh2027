Guidelines

Formatting, categories, and review process

The content here is deliberately realistic and structured so authors can scan essential instructions quickly.

Word limits and formatting

Abstracts should be prepared in clear academic English and submitted as plain text via the designated external platform. The placeholder word limit is 300 words, excluding title, authors, affiliations, and references.

Authors are encouraged to use concise background, methods, results, and conclusion language where appropriate. Final formatting rules can later specify whether structured headings are required for specific submission categories.

Review process

All submissions are assumed to undergo peer review by the scientific committee or nominated abstract assessors. Placeholder review criteria include originality, methodological clarity, relevance to the conference audience, and the likely contribution of the work to discussion at WCNH2027.

Accepted abstracts may be scheduled as oral presentations, moderated posters, or standard posters depending on reviewer scores, thematic fit, and programme balance. Final notifications can later include presentation type, timing, and presenter instructions.
