Travel

Getting here and where to stay

The travel section is organised to answer arrival, local transport, and accommodation questions in a single scroll.

By Air

Cambridge is readily reached from London Heathrow, London Stansted, and London Gatwick, with rail and coach links connecting onward to the city centre.

By Rail

Direct services from London King's Cross and London Liverpool Street make rail the most straightforward option for many UK and international delegates.

By Car

Delegates driving to Cambridge should expect limited central parking and are encouraged to use hotel parking or park-and-ride options where possible.
